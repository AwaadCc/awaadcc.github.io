About Me

A short summary about me and my goals/ambitions

I am a twenty-year-old university student majoring in Computer Science and studying for my B.S. at George Mason University. My passion for technology stems from my childhood; I began coding at around twelve, making simple websites and eventually focusing on iOS application development. I plan on graduating at the end of 2023 and, afterward, will hopefully pursue a master's in Computer Science. I love to learn more, and coding is not just my career but a genuine passion!

My fitness journey began when I was fourteen years old. Having lived a very unhealthy lifestyle I had come to the decision that it was time for a change! After spending weeks gathering information and creating my first ever program I became addicted to the grind. 5 years later and I have reached all of my original goals but as time goes on and we get older we make new goals. Thats the beauty of this journey, there is no destination because we can always be better!
